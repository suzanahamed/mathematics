Teaching Experience

I have had the privilege of working with exceptional teachers and mentors who have significantly impacted my personal and professional growth. During my tenure as a faculty member at Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology (RUET) in Bangladesh, I had the opportunity to explore various teaching roles. Additionally, my graduate studies at the University of North Texas (UNT) in Denton provided me with further exposure to teaching. Over the past ten years, I have been the instructor of record (Teaching Fellow) for numerous undergraduate courses, with class sizes ranging from 30 to 120 students. In some cases, I have also been responsible for multiple teaching assistants who assisted students in recitation/tutorial classes and graded quizzes I assigned. The courses I have taught include:

    As a Teaching Fellow at UNT

  • PreCalculus (Spring 2025, Fall 2023)
  • Calculus II (Summer 2024, Summer 2023)
  • Business Calculus (Spring 2024, Spring 2023, Fall 2022, Spring 2022)
  • College Math for Business, Economics, and Related Fields (Fall 2025, Fall 2024, Fall 2021, Spring 2021, Fall 2020)

    As a Teaching Assistant at UNT

  • PreCalculus (Fall 2019)
  • Calculus I (Spring 2020)
  • Calculus II (Spring 2020)

    As a lecturer/Assistant Professor at RUET [February 2012 to July 2019]

  • Linear Algebra
  • College Algebra
  • Numerical Analysis
  • Introduction to Matrix Theory
  • Fourier and Laplace Transforms
  • Differential and Integral Calculus
