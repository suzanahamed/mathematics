Academic and Merit Awards

Throughout my academic journey, I have received several prestigious mathematics awards recognizing my dedication and excellence. One of the most notable awards was the Professor Basak Award (2008), given to the student with the highest CGPA in the Bachelor of Science examination across thirteen departments of the College of Science at the University of Rajshahi, Bangladesh. Each award reflects effort, passion, and resilience, reminding me that academic success is as much about the journey as it is about the destination.

COS Summer Research Stipend: Summer 2025

Awarded by the College of Science (COS). The assistantship is intended to support outstanding graduates to focus on their dissertation research prior to graduation.

Professor Basak Award: B.Sc. 2008

Awarded for achieving the highest CGPA in the Bachelor of Science examination in 2008, among thirteen departments in the College of Science at the University of Rajshahi.

Agrani Bank Gold Medal Award: M.Sc. 2009

Awarded for achieving the highest CGPA in the Master of Science examination in 2009, among thirteen departments in the College of Science at the University of Rajshahi.
